Brand Operation
Description

Brand Operation undertakes online and offline brand image management and output for 021. Duties of this department include early market research, strategy formulation, and text control and visual presentation for social platforms. We are mainly in charge of operating and maintaining WeChat official accounts, as well as other social media such as Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn; we also organize and plan offline promoting activities and on-campus interactions to expand the impact of 021 in our community.
