About Us

We Are

零贰壹，Chinese Entrepreneurs Group，一个创立于多伦多大学的学生组织，旨在为多大同学们提供一个创业、职业发展和中国传统文化交流平台。

We are dedicated to fostering and promoting Chinese art, culture, thoughts, and spirit. As an entrepreneur club, we are focused on holding events that bring professional insights, corporation environment to students.
