Projects & Events

ProTalk

ProTalk is a seminar hosted by 021 club where U of T students from different faculties share and discuss their ideas and opinions on different topics. Discussions are based on topics from relevant materials prepared before the ProTalk, as well as hands-on experience in real-life situations.

Firm Visit

Firm Visit是对每一个在校大学生一种全新的体验。它能让学生去不同公司访问的过程中，相互交流，了解到很多在课堂上就学不到的，更加详细的企业 运营的实际经验，如供应链，政企关系，企业管理等一些列实际操作的规模，技巧和手段。更重要的是能亲身体会初创公司的氛围与实际的工作状态，而不 仅仅只是停留在一些文字资料甚至不知消息来源的口口相传上。Firm Visit 能为未来的真实的工作扫清不必要的环境障碍，也能为将来走向社会打下坚 实的基础。

LensWhale

LensWhale is an all-in-one app platform that connects photographers, models and those who require professional photography services. Version 1.0 is scheduled to be launched on both iOS and Android platforms by the end of 2019. LensWhale also has social and retail features that can be used to discuss all topics related to photography, as well as purchase photography related products.
