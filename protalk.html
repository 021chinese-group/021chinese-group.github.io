Protalk

ProTalk is a seminar hosted by 021 club where U of T students from different faculties share and discuss their ideas and opinions on different topics. Discussions are based on topics from relevant materials prepared before the ProTalk, as well as hands-on experience in real-life situations. Participants will discuss all the problems involved in the topic, present potential ideas and solutions and describe their thought process. All the topics are derived from the real problems that were encountered in the development of projects or professional topics from their respective industry. Discussions are designed to help you solve or find solutions to problems and improve your thought process and critical thinking skills. Each session of ProTalk will have no more than 10 participants to give everyone the opportunity to share their ideas and opinions.
